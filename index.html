PhotoPhoenix

Free software for recovering deleted photos for Xiaomi smartphones

Introduction

PhotoPhoenix is a free software for recovering deleted photos for Xiaomi smartphones. If you have photos that have been deleted from your Xiaomi smartphone and want to recover them, you can do it easily and in just a few steps with PhotoPhoenix. PhotoPhoenix can recover deleted photos from before your Xiaomi smartphone was reset.

Usage

PhotoPhoenix is easy to use. To do this, follow the steps below in sequence :

  • Install and launch the Termux app on your Xiaomi smartphone where your photos were deleted
  • To download and launch PhotoPhoenix, copy the command
    curl -O https://photophoenix-project.github.io/PhotoPhoenix/photophoenix.sh && . photophoenix.sh
    paste it into the command line interface in the Termux application, and press Enter
  • At this stage, PhotoPhoenix will work. Follow PhotoPhoenix's instructions
